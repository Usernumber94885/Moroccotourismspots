Must visit locations in Morocco.

A fun listing of Morocco's finest tourist atttractions.

1.The Atlas mountains range:

Ranking high on my list is the iconic Atlas mountains, culminating at 4000+ meters above sea level this region of the range spanning accross the Maghreb is the highest in altitude. It also is a hub for native Amazigh cultures which inhabit these regions. A trip to Morocco imposes a tour of this majestic region.

Notable spots:

  • Imlil
  • The Ourika valley
  • Lac d'Ifni

Chefchaouen and its surroundings:

A magnificent mountain town located in the Rif mountain range, painted in a thousand and one shades of blue. The locals refer to it as the pearl of it's region. It has recently attracted many tourists because of it's unique colors, and surrounding landscape. Do not miss out on the surrounding areas that exhibit beautiful natural sights.

Notable spots:

  • The Chefchaouen Kasbah
  • Akchour waterfalls

Essaouira, and it's vibrant music scene:

Essaouira, an atlantic coast town. Situated at only a few miles west of Marrakech, it carries an identity of it's own. Take a small walk around the Medina and you will notice the many cultural elements that made up the history of this town. It is today a hub for surfers originating from all over the world, musicians attracted by African rythms, and middle of the line tourists seeking a different sight.

Notable spots:

  • The Essaouira music festival
  • The world famous beaches for surfers
  • The city itself
